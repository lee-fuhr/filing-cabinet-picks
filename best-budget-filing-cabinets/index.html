Quick answer

For a no‑nonsense, budget‑friendly filing cabinet that doesn’t feel flimsy, the Lorell 18″ Deep 2‑Drawer Vertical File Cabinet is the clear top pick.

Paper isn’t going away, and neither is the need for a sturdy file cabinet that keeps invoices, tax records, and client folders organized without busting your budget. The trick is finding a model that combines solid construction with a price tag that doesn’t make you wince, especially when you’re outfitting a home office or a growing small business. When shopping on a budget, focus on three things: drawer slide quality (full‑extension ball‑bearing slides are a plus), build material (steel holds up better than MDF over years of use), and the filing system you already own. Vertical cabinets are narrower and stack well in tight corners; lateral cabinets hold side‑to‑side hanging folders and offer more filing width. Mobile pedestals add flexibility, letting you roll files right under your desk. We’ve researched owner feedback and expert reviews to find the best options at each price level.
