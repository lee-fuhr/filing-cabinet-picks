A good filing cabinet remains the backbone of any organized home office or small business. Even in a digital age, paper documents, contracts, tax records, medical forms, still pile up, and a dedicated cabinet keeps them secure, sorted, and within easy reach. The challenge is choosing between lateral and vertical styles, single- and multi-drawer setups, and materials that range from budget laminate to all-steel commercial grade. When shopping, think first about the footprint you can spare: lateral cabinets are wide and shallow, perfect beside a desk, while vertical units are narrow but deep, fitting tight corners. Drawer suspension, lock quality, and weight capacity matter more than looks if you plan to fill the cabinet to its limit. Mobile cabinets add flexibility but sacrifice some stability and capacity.
